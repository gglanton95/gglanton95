Mexican Street Corn Chicken and Rice Bowls

Cookbook

Ingredients

  • 3-4 Chicken Breasts
  • 4 Cups of Cooked Rice
  • 2 Cups of Corn
  • 1/2 Cup of Diced Red Onions
  • 1/3 Cup of Mayo
  • 2 tbs of Lime Juice
  • 1 tbs of Minced Garlic
  • 2 tsp of Chili Powder
  • 2 tsp of Paprika
  • 1 tbs Garlic Powder
  • Salt and Pepper for Taste
  • 1/2 Cup Cojita Cheese Toppings
  • Additional Toppings of Choice- cilantro, sour cream, avacado, cherry tomatoes, beans, tajine.

Directions

  1. Combine 1 tbs Chili Powder, 1 tsp Paprika, 1 tbs Garlic Powder, salt and pepper. Season Chicken with seasoning mixture.
  2. Place Seasoned Chicken in a backing dish and cook in oven at 400 degrees 20-25 minutes, or until internal temperature reaches 165 degrees.
  3. Cut chicken into stripes or cube it- your choice.
  4. Combine cooked corn, mayo, garlic, remaining seasoning, lime juice, salt and pepper, red onion, and cojita cheese in a bowl. Mix well.
  5. Divide rice between bowls before adding the chicken and the street corn salad.
  6. Top with any additional cilantro or toppings you may like.
